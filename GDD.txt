Game Title: CopyMaster 3D 
Genre: 3D Stealth / Action 
Platform: PC 
Engine: Unity 
Target Audience: 12+, players who enjoy strategy and stealth

1. Game Concept

Players attempt to cheat off opponents’ work or exams without being caught. The goal is to achieve the highest score or reward while avoiding detection.

2. Core Gameplay Mechanics

2.1 Character Controls

Movement: WASD or joystick for walking/running

Camera/View: Mouse to look around

Interaction: E key / button to copy or interact with objects

2.2 Cheating Mechanic

Player can cheat by observing opponents’ exam sheets from a specific distance.

Risk System: Looking too long increases chance of being caught by teacher or rival players.

2.3 Stealth & Detection

Player is safe when in shadows or out of enemies’ line of sight.

If detected: warning issued or penalty points applied.

2.4 Scoring System

Successful cheat: +10 points

Getting caught: -5 points

First to finish objective: +20 points

3. Game World / Levels

Classroom Level: Small, simple area with desks and teacher desk

School Level: Multiple classrooms and corridors, more hiding spots

4. Characters

Teacher: AI-controlled, limited vision cone, triggers detection mechanics

5. Art & Audio Design

Graphics: Simple and colorful 3D, low-poly or semi-realistic style

Animations: Walking, running, looking, cheating, getting caught

Sound: Whispering, footsteps, teacher alerts, background music

6. Technology & Tools

Engine: Unity 3D (C#)

Animation: Mixamo 

7. Gameplay Loop

Player enters the classroom

Observes opponents and searches for cheating opportunities

Evaluates risk of being caught

Cheats or completes objectives

Gains or loses points
